<p><strong>Shape Your Future at Dunman Secondary School</strong>
</p>
<p>&nbsp;Do you possess a specialized talent in <strong>Sports, Aesthetics, Leadership, or STEM?</strong> We
invite all interested Primary 6 students to apply for the Direct School
Admission (DSA) exercise for the 2027 academic year.</p>
<p>&nbsp;We encourage parents and students to choose a pathway that aligns
with the student’s genuine aptitudes and strengths. When applying, please
consider our school’s academic and non-academic environment, as well as
the specialised programmes we offer to nurture your specific talents.</p>
<p>&nbsp;Students admitted through the DSA exercise are expected to honour
their commitment to Dunman Secondary. This includes active participation
in all activities and developmental programmes related to their talent
area from <strong>Year 1 through Year 4</strong>.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><strong>Selection Considerations &amp; Criteria</strong>
</p>
<p><strong>1. General Selection Process</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>CCA Track Record:</strong> Evaluation will be based on the student's
primary school CCA records; <strong>no hard-copy submission is required</strong> at
the initial stage.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Shortlisting:</strong> Only shortlisted applicants will be notified
of the specific date and time for their trials, auditions, or interviews.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Supporting Documents (Optional):</strong> While not mandatory,
you may choose to email additional supporting documents to the relevant
Teacher-in-Charge (refer to the contact list below).</p>
</li>
</ul>
<hr>
<p><strong>2. Talent-Specific Shortlisting Criteria</strong>
</p>
<p>To be considered for shortlisting, applicants should meet the following
requirements:</p>
<p><strong>Sports &amp; Games</strong>
</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Consistent participation
in a Sports CCA in Primary School.</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Regular involvement
in competitive sports or organized games outside of school.</p>
<p><strong>Performing Arts</strong>
</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Active participation
in a Performing Arts CCA in Primary School.</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Consistent involvement
in Performing Arts programmes or performances outside of school.</p>
<p>&nbsp;<strong>STEM</strong>
</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Active participation
in a STEM-related CCA in Primary School.</p>
<p>•&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Participation in STEM-related
competitions, workshops, or programmes outside of school.</p>
<p><strong>Leadership (Must meet ALL criteria)</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Held a recognized school-based leadership position (e.g., Prefect, CCA
Leader, Class Monitor).</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Demonstrated active contribution to school and/or community events.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Consistently maintained <strong>Exemplary Conduct</strong> grades.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Important Notes for Applicants</strong>
</p>
<p>a)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; Meeting all criteria does not guarantee the student
will be shortlisted/given an offer.</p>
<p>b)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; If your child has been shortlisted to undergo Direct
School Admission (DSA) selection, you will receive notification within
the stipulated period.</p>
<p>&nbsp;For more details, please refer to MOE website – <a href="" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">www.moe.gov.sg/dsa-sec</a>
</p>
