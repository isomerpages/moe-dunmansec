<h3>Introduction</h3>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/CCA%20Photos/Volleyball/Picture1.jpg">
</div>
<p>Dunman's Volleyball CCA provides a vibrant and enriching experience for
our student athletes. We value competitive sports for the lessons they
teach about leadership, teamwork, victory and defeat. In addition to volleyball
skills and knowledge, we constantly seek to provide students in the programme
with a supportive and engaging environment to develop academically, socially
and emotionally as well.</p>
<p>Our training programme is structured with technical skills training and
a strong focus on inculcating character-building values.</p>
<p></p>
<p>This includes:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Emphasis on skills development</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Meticulous tactical training</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Customised physical conditioning</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Individual academic support</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Integration of Social-Emotional Learning through Sportsmanship and Leadership</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
